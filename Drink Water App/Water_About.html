About Water





Water makes up about 60% of your body. So making sure that you are hydrated is a must. Some benefits drinking water are: It increases energy & relieves fatigue, promotes weight loss, flushes out toxins, boosts immune system, natural headache remedy and prevents cramps & sprains. What our application does is it reminds the user to drink a certain amount of water at certain points during the day to stay hydrated.
